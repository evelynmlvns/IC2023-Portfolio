About Me

My name is Evelyn Luevanos and I am currently a rising Senior at Speedway High School.

I was born in California but was raised in Indiana alongside my two younger sisters by my mom and abuelita.

I owe everything to my mom and abuelita because they have given up so much in order to provide for me and my sisters by themselves.

Growing up as the first-born daughter of a single mother has taught me the harsh reality of life at such a young age. But I aspire to one day be an example to young hispanic females.
